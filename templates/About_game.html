Welcome in The Ball Game!

A game of soccer to a single player game. Colorful balls pop in random slots for blueprints after each move. Apparently you are arranging three balls of the same color on one line. If you can do it You gain extra traffic. The game continues until all boxes are on the game boards will be filled with marbles. You can choose from four levels.

Easy

The simplest level, the balls appear individually in 3 colors

Medium

Level a bit harder than Easy. Although the balls appear singly, they have 4 colors

Hard

The most difficult level in which only one ball appears at a time. There are already 5 ball colors here

Expert

For real experts on The Ball Game. The appearing balls have 5 colors. At this level, as many as 3 balls appear after your every move. Think carefully about every move, one mistake can end the game
